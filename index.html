Our Story

Founded in 2024, Krypto is a Fortnite organization primarily known for its content, built around consistency, personality, and relevance within the scene.

Alongside content, Krypto fielded competitive rosters that qualified for every FNCS Grand Finals we competed in. We approached competition with structure and intent, without positioning it as the sole identity of the organization.

After being inactive for over a year, Krypto resumed operations in 2026 with a clearer direction and tighter focus. The organization now operates with long-term intent across content and competition, prioritizing quality, sustainability, and credibility.
